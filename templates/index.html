什么是八字？

八字，又称四柱命理，是中国古老的命理学体系，已有千年历史。通过分析出生年、月、日、时的天干地支组合，揭示个人性格特征、人生走向和运势变化。

四柱详解

年柱

月柱
Month Pillar

日柱

What is BaZi?

BaZi (八字), also known as the Four Pillars of Destiny, is an ancient Chinese astrological system with over 1,000 years of history. By analyzing the Heavenly Stems and Earthly Branches of your birth year, month, day, and hour, it reveals personality traits, life patterns, and fortune cycles.

The Four Pillars

YEAR
Year Pillar
Ancestry, early childhood, social influence. First impressions.
MONTH
Month Pillar
Parents, career potential, character foundation.
DAY
Day Pillar
Your true self, personality core, marriage. The center of the chart.
HOUR
Hour Pillar
Children, creativity, legacy, later years.

Five Elements

🌳 Wood 🔥 Fire 🌍 Earth ⚔️ Metal 💧 Water
